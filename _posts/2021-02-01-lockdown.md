Life has changed a lot for most of us in 2020 and it seems that it will continue for at least another year.

To adapt to these new constraints, I've focussed on doing things I've always loved (programming, Xbox, Netflix!) and a few new things to mix it up...

## Music

![Photo of a person playing an acoustic guitar](/images/guitar.jpg)

Picking up the guitar again after a few years has been a really nice way of creating space for creativity.

## Sprechen Sie Deutsch?

![Photo of a sign post including various languages](/images/language.jpg)

Spending time in Europe I've made the commitment to learn German which is both challenging and rewarding. I look forward to some practice once we are able to travel again.

## Movement and focus

![Photo of black and blue rolled up yoga mats in a shelf](/images/yoga.jpg)

Without a commute there is much less day-to-day activity (desk to fridge to couch isn't enough!). Even though its not something that comes natural, I've found it really helpful to set aside time for excercise, stretching and meditation.
